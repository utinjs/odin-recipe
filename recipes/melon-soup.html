Melon Soup Recipe

Description

Melon soup is a proud dish of the Akwa Ibom people of Nigeria. The dish is famed for it's nation-wide acceptance by people of diverse tribes and tongue.

According to data gathered across Nigeria, people prefer eating it with fufu (a highly fermented bi-product of cassava). But it can also be consumed with garri.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Stock
  • Grinded melon seeds
  • Vegetable leave of choice
  • Removed Periwinkle and other freshies
  • Palm oil

Steps

  1. Allow your stocks to cook properly
  2. Add palm oil and allow it mix in properly
  3. Add your periwinkle and other freshies
  4. Add your melon and allow it cook for a while
  5. Add your vegetables of choice and allow to cook for 1-2mins and dish
