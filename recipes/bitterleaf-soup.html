Bitterleaf Soup Recipe

Description

bitterleaf soup is a proud dish of the of the Nigerian people. The dish is famed for it's nation-wide acceptance by people of diverse tribes and tongue.

According to data gathered across Nigeria, people prefer eating it with fufu (a highly fermented bi-product of cassava). But it can also be consumed with garri.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Stock
  • Washed bitterleaf
  • Freshies of choice
  • Palm oil

Steps

  1. Allow your stocks to cook properly
  2. Add palm oil and allow it mix in properly
  3. Add your freshies
  4. Add your bitterleaf and allow to cook for 2-3mins and dish
